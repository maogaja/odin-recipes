Shrimp Marinara

Description

Shrimp marinara hits all the right spots - tasty shrimp with homemade tomato sauce served over spaghetti. A quick and easy weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • Pasta:This shrimp marinara recipe starts with dry spaghetti noodles.
  • Oil:Cook the shrimp in extra-virgin olive oil.
  • Shrimp:You will need a pound of peeled and deveined shrimp.
  • Seasoning:This dish is seasoned with fresh garlic, crushed red pepper, fresh basil, dried oregano, and kosher salt.
  • Canned Tomatoes.The marinara sauce calls for canned fire-roasted tomatoes and canned tomato sauce.
  • Balsamic Vinegar: Add bold flavor and brightness with a tablespoon of balsamic vinegar.

Steps

  1. Make the spaghetti.
  2. Cook the shrimp and transfer to a plate.
  3. Stir in the remaining ingredients and bring to a boil, then simmer until thickened.
  4. Return the shrimp to the skillet and cook until heated through.
  5. Serve over spaghetti and garnish with fresh basil.
